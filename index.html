Hi! I am going to spend a few minutes to give you an understanding of the relationship between the Product Age and the Consumer Experience. To Win with Taste in the marketplace, is crucial to the success of the Coca-Cola Hellenic business. It is a hard fact that Product Age affects both the taste and the appearance of our beverages negatively. It is therefore critical to manage Product Age within Primary Age. But what is Primary Age, I hear you ask. And why is it so important to us? Primary Age is the length of time within which the beverage under controlled conditions delivers the designed taste and appearance. It is an important metric we target to keep our promise delivering consumer satisfaction on every occasion. Total Age Management, also known as TAM, is the structured end to end process we use to ensure our products are fresh and consumed within the Primary Age. To give you a real life example, here I have 2 bottles of Pulpy juice products. One is within the Primary Age. And the other one is beyond Primary Age. And I assume you can guess which is which. The one on my left side is beyond Primary Age and, as you can see, the colour has significantly faded. And take it from me, the taste has also changed and not for the better. TAM is the responsibility of everyone. Its success is dependent on cross-functional accountability, all of us working together. Only this end to end approach, from forecasting accuracy to commercial execution will make the necessary change. But why is this important to each of us? Simply put, to ensure consumer satisfaction, which in return drives sustainable growth for Coca-Cola Hellenic and our success.
